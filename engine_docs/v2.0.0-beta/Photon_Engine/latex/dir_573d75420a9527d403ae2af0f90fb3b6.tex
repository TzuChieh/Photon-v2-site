\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Common functionalities and definitions for the engine. 

For developers, try to avoid any use of other parts of the engine for implementation here. This part of the engine is fairly low-\/level and referencing other parts of the engine may introduce undesirable dependencies or worse, a cyclic dependency.
