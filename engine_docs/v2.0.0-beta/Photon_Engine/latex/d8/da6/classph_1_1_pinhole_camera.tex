Generate a ray received by the receiver. 

Given raster coordinates, generate a corresponding ray that would have hit that location from the light entry of the receiver. The light entry would be the outmost surface of a camera\textquotesingle{}s lens system for example. This method is for Monte-\/\+Carlo sampling.

\begin{DoxyReturn}{Returns}
A weighting factor for the received quantity. Proper sensor response and sampling PDF are included in the returned weight. 
\end{DoxyReturn}
