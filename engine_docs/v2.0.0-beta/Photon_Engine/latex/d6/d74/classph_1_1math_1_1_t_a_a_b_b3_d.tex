A 3-\/D Axis-\/\+Aligned Bounding Box. 

A construct that have a cuboid-\/shaped boundary. An AABB, depending on the usage, may represent a point, a layer of skin, or a volume.

Checks whether a line segment intersects this volume. 

Returned boolean value indicates whether the ray is intersecting with the AABB\textquotesingle{}s volume or not. If there\textquotesingle{}s an intersection, the near and far hit distances will be returned via (out\+\_\+ray\+Near\+Hit\+Dist, out\+\_\+ray\+Far\+Hit\+Dist); if the starting point of the ray is inside the AABB, near hit distance will be the ray\textquotesingle{}s t\+\_\+min since volume intersection starts immediately on the ray\textquotesingle{}s starting point (the ending point of the ray follows the same logic).
