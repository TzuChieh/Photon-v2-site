\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Modeling Fresnel effects that occur on interfaces. 

The two sides of a interface are called inner and outer sides, where outer side has \$\textbackslash{}cos(\textbackslash{}theta\+\_\+\{indicent\})\texorpdfstring{$>$}{>}0\$ and inner side has \$\textbackslash{}cos(\textbackslash{}theta\+\_\+\{indicent\})\texorpdfstring{$<$}{<}0\$.
