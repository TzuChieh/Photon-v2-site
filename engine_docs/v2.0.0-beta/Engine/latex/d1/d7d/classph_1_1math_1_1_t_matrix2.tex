Solve N linear systems of the form Ax = b. 

Treating this matrix as A, linear systems A\texorpdfstring{$\ast$}{*}x\+\_\+i = b\+\_\+i (0 \texorpdfstring{$<$}{<}= i \texorpdfstring{$<$}{<} N) are solved. This is equivalent to having {\ttfamily bs} as a {\ttfamily 2} by {\ttfamily N} matrix and passing them in column-\/major ({\ttfamily out\+\_\+xs} follows similarly, except it\textquotesingle{}s for outputs).


\begin{DoxyParams}[1]{Parameters}
 & {\em A} & A 2x2 matrix. \\
\hline
 & {\em bs} & {\ttfamily N} 2x1 vectors (or a 2xN matrix in column-\/major). \\
\hline
\mbox{\texttt{ out}}  & {\em out\+\_\+xs} & {\ttfamily N} 2x1 vectors (or a 2xN matrix in column-\/major). \\
\hline
\end{DoxyParams}
\begin{DoxyReturn}{Returns}
