\doxysection*{Photon Renderer}

\doxyparagraph*{{\itshape A physically based render engine for ultra realistic image synthesis.}}

\hypertarget{index_autotoc_md0}{}\doxysubsection{\texorpdfstring{A Brief Introduction}{A Brief Introduction}}\label{index_autotoc_md0}
Photon is a rendering engine made out of my personal interest in computer graphics. A rendering engine is what takes geometrical descriptions and material properties of a 3-\/D scene as input, and outputs a 2-\/D image of that scene from a specific point of view. In layman\textquotesingle{}s terms, it is like taking a photograph, except the scene and the camera are both simulated by a computer. This project is actively being developed and more features are expected to come.
